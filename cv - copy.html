Research

Multi-Messenger Measurement of Hubble's Constant 2019 - ongoing

I am leading a multi-institution, global observing campaign of gravitational wave events to observe optical counterparts to neutron star mergers. The end goal is to numerically model these observations to definitively measure the expansion rate of the universe and to better understand the origin of the densest elements in the universe such as gold and uranium.

Radio Mapping Algorithm 2015 - 2018

I developed a radio mapping algorithm in C++ that contaminant cleans, maps, and photometers radio astronomy data. Routinely collaborated in development trips to push before deadlines. I am currently integrating the algorithm into Skynet so that it will automatically process all data taken from Skynet radio telescopes around the world.
